Our Mission
The BOSS team uses its mix of operational, administrative, and system knowledge to support existing system usage and to coordinate the needs for successful new system implementation by beiding the gap between system development and users from different North American sites.

News and Events

At Katoen Natie, our people make the difference, and BOSS team is helping improve things for the people, one project at a time!. Here is a list of current major projects!

  • eOrder is back online and almost every site and customer is using it! We will continue to monitor for any issues, but so far everything is looking great!

  • Invoicing in Mexico is getting a complete overhaul. Mariel of sales and our very own Alexis Artiga has been working hard to clean up the database and get things organized for a better and smoother invoicing process.

    • *The goal is for all the sites to get a good clean up and overhaul
    so that the invoicing process can be better and faster for everyone.


  • Dashboards are growing! A new supplies dashboard has been created and an invoicing dashboard is soon to join! Storing and displaying large amounts of useful and relevant data is important to better productivity and our ability to asses where issues lie. Take some time to look through the dashboard system, you might find data on things you never knew before!

  • Exxonlubes and Exxonbutyl Rubber are getting some updates to their process. Butyl has started RF scanning on all their product and Lubes is starting to use the new
    Put-Away Strategy The "Put-Away" is a setup in Plato used to automatically assign locations for product from the inbound.
    system.
